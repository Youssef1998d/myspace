ENTP type of person ?

For me a personality is hard to describe as everyone is unique the way he thinks, behaves and acts because, everything is based on a set of morals, memories, beliefs that are different from one to another, although, we might be similar in general things. So, the MBTI test suggests that all people can be grouped in 16 different personalities in which every group have similarities in the way they thinks, perceives, acts, etc .. And that is done by differenciating people with 4 pairs of preferences which are : Extraversion or Introversion, Sensing or Intuition, Thinking or Feeling and Judging or Perceiving. You can learn more about MBTI test in this link.

There is so many free MBTI tests that you can find online, and they are quick to pass. But in order to get a credible result, you might have to take a paid one. In my situation, i took the MBTI test as part of personal developement training and internship preparation at Ecole Supérieure Léonard de Vinci in Paris while my exchange semester. So i passed the official test guided by a MBTI instrument administrator, and my result was ENTP! So what is it ?

ENTP type head

ENTP strengths

ENTPs solve problems creatively and are often innovative in their way of thinking, seeing connections and patterns within a system. They enjoy developing strategy and often spot and capitalise on new opportunities that present themselves.

ENTP type down-head

Potential development areas for ENTPs

ENTPs sometimes avoid making decisions and may become excited about ideas that are not feasible because of constraints on time or resources. They may be overly challenging to others and their ideas.
